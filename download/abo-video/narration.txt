ABO: The Autonomous Business Operating System.

Five specialized AI agents that automate your entire business pipeline. From lead qualification, through client onboarding, delivery monitoring, finance operations, and knowledge management. All orchestrated through a single workflow engine with human-in-the-loop approvals, audit trails, and automatic retry logic.

Here is the core problem. Growing businesses run on dozens of disconnected tools. Leads come in through forms, get enriched in Apollo, scored in spreadsheets, pushed to HubSpot, then someone manually creates Notion pages, Jira tickets, calendar invites, and Slack notifications. Client onboarding alone takes five different tools and two days. Finance ops means switching between Stripe, QuickBooks, and email. And when something goes wrong, there is no single audit trail. Every step is manual, every handoff is fragile, and nothing is automated.

ABO solves this with five domain-specific agents managed by a Master Orchestrator. A new lead hits your webhook. The Lead Qualification Agent enriches it via Apollo, verifies the email with Hunter, scores it A through C, drafts a personalized outreach email, and syncs it to HubSpot or Salesforce. If it is a Tier A lead, the workflow pauses for human approval before sending. Once the contract is signed, the Client Onboarding Agent takes over. It creates a Notion page, spins up Linear or Jira tasks, schedules a kickoff meeting, and posts to your Slack channel. During delivery, the Delivery Monitoring Agent watches for budget drift, communication gaps, and schedule delays. If risks are detected, it creates escalations for your ops team. The Finance Operations Agent creates invoices, checks for overdue payments, reconciles accounts, and generates weekly summaries. And the Knowledge Agent ingests meeting summaries and answers questions from your institutional knowledge base.

Every tool call goes through a single instrumented execute_tool method that automatically creates task records, normalizes results, and writes to the audit trail. Every integration adapter has safe simulation mode, meaning the entire system runs end-to-end with zero API keys. You can demo the full pipeline locally with just Docker Compose. When you are ready for production, swap in real credentials for Apollo, Hunter, HubSpot, Stripe, Notion, Slack, QuickBooks, DocuSign, Linear, or Google Calendar.

The admin dashboard gives your operations team full visibility. Workflow history, task traces, approval queues, and audit logs, all in one place. Prometheus metrics, structured JSON logging, and health and readiness probes are built in from day one.

ABO is twenty-nine hundred lines of production Python. Just fourteen dependencies. No ML frameworks, no message queues, no frontend build step. It is the leanest, most complete autonomous business system you can deploy today.
